01 // WHAT IS HUMANITAI CIC?

A new kind of local support infrastructure.

HumanitAI CIC is a UK Community Interest Company serving Bath & North East Somerset and beyond. We exist to improve physical, mental, social, and digital wellbeing through community-based prevention.

People need more than services when they are already breaking down. They need places, support, and connection earlier. We help people feel healthier, calmer, and better able to function in everyday life.

02 // THE CHALLENGE

Why this matters.

Many people do not need a clinical intervention first — they need support that helps them reconnect with themselves and others. Too often, people fall through the gap between isolation and crisis.

  • Socially isolated adults
  • Long-term stress or low confidence
  • Excluded from mainstream wellbeing spaces
  • Struggling with routine or daily functioning
  • Digitally excluded or unsure of modern tools
03 // WHAT WE DO

Integrated Community Model.

06 // Ethical AI & Digital

Building confidence with modern tools in safe, human-centered ways. Reducing the gap caused by AI exclusion.
