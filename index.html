01 // THE MISSION

What is HumanitAI?

We are a UK Community Interest Company. We exist to improve physical, mental, and digital wellbeing through community-based prevention. People need more than services when they're already breaking down—they need places and support earlier.

Prevention First

Building local infrastructure that helps people feel healthier and calmer before they reach crisis point.

Ethical AI

Technology that supports rather than replaces. Closing the digital exclusion gap safely.

02 // THE CHALLENGE

Closing the gap.

The Problem

Loneliness, chronic stress, and digital exclusion are rising. Many people don't need a clinical intervention first—they need connection.

Who We Support

Socially isolated adults, unpaid carers, disabled residents, neurodivergent individuals, and the digitally excluded.
